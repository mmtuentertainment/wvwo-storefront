# Burnsville Lake WMA Map

The official WV DNR map for Burnsville Lake WMA. Covers the full 12,000+ acres of public hunting land.

## What's on the Map

- All access points and parking areas
- Hunting zone boundaries
- Lake shoreline and boat ramps
- Trail systems
