# Riffle Run Campground

60 campsites on the dam side of Burnsville Lake. Smaller and quieter than Bulltown. Mix of full hookup and primitive tent sites at lower price points.

## What Makes It Special

- **Tailwater trout fishing** below the dam (stocked in spring)
- **Lower prices** than Bulltown - primitives from $12/night
- **First-come, first-served** sites available
- **Marina access** nearby at Burnsville Docks
- **Quieter atmosphere** - popular with anglers

## Campsites

| Type | Sites | Nightly Fee |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Full Hookup | 54 | $28 |
| Primitive (Tent) | 6 | $12 |

**Season:** Mid-April through late November

## Nearby Activities

- **Tailwater Trout Fishing** - WVDNR stocks rainbow trout in spring (below dam, walking distance)
- **Burnsville Docks / Marina** - pontoon and jet ski rentals, boat slips
- **Burnsville Lake Fishing** - walleye, saugeye, largemouth bass, catfish
- **Burnsville Lake WMA** - 12,579 acres of public hunting land

## Reservations

- **Most sites:** First-come, first-served (no reservation required)
- **Select sites:** Book via Recreation.gov

**Recreation.gov ID:** 10234906
**USACE Office:** (304) 853-2371
