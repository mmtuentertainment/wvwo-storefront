Snowshoe Mountain is West Virginia's premier destination ski resort, offering an authentic mountain experience with 60 trails spread across 257 skiable acres. Rising to 4,848 feet, it's one of the highest points in the state, ensuring reliable snow conditions and stunning views of the Allegheny Highlands.

The resort features terrain for all skill levels, from gentle beginner slopes at Ballhooter to the challenging double-black diamond runs on the Western Territory. With 14 lifts including three high-speed quads, you'll spend more time skiing and less time waiting. The 100% snowmaking coverage means consistent conditions throughout the season.

Beyond skiing, Snowshoe transforms into a summer adventure destination with world-class mountain biking, the acclaimed Raven Golf Club, and miles of hiking trails. Whether you're carving fresh corduroy at dawn or enjoying aprés-ski at The Boathouse, Snowshoe delivers a complete mountain resort experience just 2.5 hours from our shop.
