# Gerald R. Freeman Campground

The largest campground on Sutton Lake with 156 sites ranging from full hookups to electric-only. Named after a longtime USACE employee, this campground offers modern amenities in a wooded lakeside setting.

## What Makes It Special

- **156 campsites** - largest on Sutton Lake
- **Full hookups available** (water, electric, sewer)
- **30 and 50 amp service** for RVs
- **Boat ramp on-site** - direct lake access
- **Hot showers** in multiple bathhouses
- **Playgrounds and basketball courts**
- **Universally accessible sites**

## Critical Information

**CELL SERVICE IS LIMITED** - Verizon has the best coverage in Braxton County at 68.4%, but expect spotty service at the campground. Download offline maps and let someone know your plans before you arrive.

**Quiet Hours:** 10 PM - 6 AM
**Stay Limit:** 14 days per 30-day period
**Pets:** Allowed on leash

## Nearby Activities

- **Sutton Lake fishing** - Walleye, bass, crappie, catfish (20-30" walleye slot limit)

- **Hiking** - Trails throughout Elk River WMA
