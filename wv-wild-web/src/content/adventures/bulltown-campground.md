# Bulltown Campground

204 campsites on the shore of Burnsville Lake in Braxton County. The largest campground in the I-79 corridor between Charleston and Morgantown.

## What Makes It Special

- **Full hookups** on 134 sites (50 amp, water, sewer)
- **Civil War history** - adjacent to Bulltown Historic Area battlefield
- **Boat ramp** right at the campground
- **Playgrounds** in every loop
- **No cell service** - bring offline maps

## Campsites

| Type | Sites | Nightly Fee |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Full Hookup (50 Amp) | 134 | $34-$46 |
| Electric Only (50 Amp) | 48 | $34-$40 |
| Electric Only (30 Amp) | 22 | $30-$36 |

**Season:** Mid-April through late November

## Nearby Activities

- **Bulltown Historic Area** - Civil War battlefield, museum, interpretive trails (walking distance)
- **Swimming Beach** - 1 mile at day-use area
- **Burnsville Lake Fishing** - largemouth bass, walleye, crappie, muskellunge
- **Burnsville Lake WMA** - 12,579 acres of public hunting land

## Reservations

- **Peak Season (Memorial Day - Labor Day):** Book via Recreation.gov
- **Off-Peak:** First-come, first-served
