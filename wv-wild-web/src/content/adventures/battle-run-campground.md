# Battle Run Campground

117 campsites on a peninsula jutting into Summersville Lake - West Virginia's largest lake and the "Little Bahamas of the East." Crystal-clear water with 20-45 feet visibility makes this unlike any other camping experience in the state.

## What Makes It Special

- **1,000-foot swimming beach** with crystal-clear water
- **110 electric sites** (30 amp) with gravel pads up to 50 ft
- **7 tent-only walk-in sites** for a more secluded experience
- **Boat ramp and kayak launch** on-site
- **3 shower houses** with hot water and laundry
- **NO PETS** - strictly enforced (unlike most USACE campgrounds)

**Season:** May 1 - Columbus Day
**Max RV Length:** 50 feet
**Max Occupancy:** 8 people, 3 vehicles per site

## Nearby Activities

- **Summersville Lake** - 2,790 acres of crystal-clear fishing for bass, walleye, catfish
- **Scuba Diving** - Sarge's Dive Shop at Long Point Marina offers certifications and charters
- **Summersville Lake WMA** - 5,982 acres of hunting (deer, turkey, grouse, small game)
- **Salmon Run** - Year-round boat launch, day-use, trail to Pirates Cove
- **New River Gorge National Park** - 20 miles, rock climbing and whitewater

## Critical Policies

**PETS STRICTLY PROHIBITED** - No dogs, no exceptions. This is different from most USACE campgrounds. Plan accordingly or use Long Point (8 primitive sites, pets allowed).

**Quiet Hours:** 10 PM - 8 AM
**Alcohol:** Allowed at campsites, PROHIBITED at beach
**Firearms:** Prohibited on federal property

## Reservations

- **Peak Season (Memorial Day - Labor Day):** Book via Recreation.gov up to 6 months in advance
- **Off-Peak:** First-come, first-served
