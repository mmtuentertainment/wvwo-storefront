# Sutton Lake

Just 10 minutes up the road from the shop. Sutton Lake is 1,440 acres of Army Corps lake with excellent fishing and recreation.

## What to Fish

- Largemouth Bass
- Smallmouth Bass
- Channel Catfish
- Crappie
- Walleye

## Facilities

The lake has multiple boat ramps, campgrounds, and day-use areas. Swimming is allowed at designated beaches.

## Getting There

From the shop (121 WV-82):
1. Head north on I-79
2. Take Exit 62 (Sutton)
3. Follow signs to Sutton Lake
4. About 10 minutes total
