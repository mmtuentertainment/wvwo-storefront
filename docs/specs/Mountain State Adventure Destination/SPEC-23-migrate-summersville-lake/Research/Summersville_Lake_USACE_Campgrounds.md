# Summersville Lake USACE Campgrounds
## Battle Run & Salmon Run Campground Data

---

## 1. BATTLE RUN CAMPGROUND

### Location & Contact Information
- **Physical Address:** 2981 Summersville Lake Road, Summersville, WV 26651
- **GPS Coordinates:** 38.21912, -80.89541
- **Phone:** 304-872-3412 (Lake Office) / 304-872-3459 (Campground Office)
- **Email:** pa2@usace.army.mil
- **Reservations:** 1-877-444-6777 (toll-free) or Recreation.gov
- **Managed by:** U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Huntington District

### Campsite Inventory
- **Total Campsites:** 117 single-family sites
- **RV/Trailer Sites with Electric:** 110 sites (30 amp hookups)
- **Tent-Only/Walk-In Sites:** 7 primitive tent sites (150-200 feet walk from parking)
- **Maximum RV Length:** Up to 50 feet
- **Vehicles per Site:** Up to 3 vehicles per site
- **Maximum Occupancy:** 8 people per site

### Site Composition
- All 110 electric sites appear to be standard hookup sites (30 amp only - no 50 amp available)
- **Water Availability:** Water spigots available throughout campground loops
- **Hookup Types:** 
  - 110 sites: Electric only (30 amp)
  - 7 sites: No hookups (tent-only)
  - No water-only hookup sites listed
  - No full hookup sites available

### Site Configuration
- **Pull-Through vs. Back-In:** Mix of pull-through and back-in sites (specific breakdown not disclosed)
- Gravel parking pads
- Each site includes: picnic table, fire ring with grill

### Campground Amenities
- **Restrooms:** Flush toilets available
- **Shower Houses:** 3 shower houses with hot showers
- **Laundry:** Washers and dryers available (coin-operated)
- **Dump Station:** Available (located at front of campground)
- **Beach Access:** 1,000-foot public swimming beach
- **Boat Launch:** Boat ramp on-site; Kayak launch ramp available
- **Fishing Pier:** Available
- **Recreational:** Playground, basketball courts, volleyball courts, nature trails
- **Miscellaneous:** Firewood available for purchase, recycling center, trash collection

### Campground Fees (2024-2025)
- **Peak Season (Memorial Day - Labor Day):**
  - $34/night - Standard Electric (30 amp sites)
  - $24/night - Tent-Only/Non-Electric sites
  
- **Off-Season (May 1 - Memorial Day, Labor Day - Columbus Day):**
  - Same fee structure

- **Day Use Fee:** $5.00 per vehicle

### Camping Season & Reservations
- **Operating Season:** May 1 - Columbus Day (approximately May-October)
- **Peak Season Reservations:** Memorial Day weekend - Labor Day
  - All campsites reservable during peak season
  - Reservations accepted up to 6 months in advance
  - Book through Recreation.gov or 1-877-444-6777
  
- **Off-Peak Season:** Before Memorial Day and after Labor Day
  - Operated on "first come, first serve" basis
  
- **Check-In/Check-Out:**
  - Peak Season: 7:00 AM - Midnight
  - Off-Peak Season: 5:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  - Maximum stay: 14 consecutive nights per visit; no more than 14 days in any 30-day consecutive period

### Policies & Regulations
- **Pets:** STRICTLY PROHIBITED - no pets or livestock allowed
- **Quiet Hours:** 10:00 PM - 8:00 AM (standard for WV State Parks/USACE facilities)
- **Motorized Vehicles:** No ATVs, UTVs, mopeds, scooters, golf carts, or other non-licensed recreational vehicles in campground
- **Firewood:** Do not move firewood from outside area; purchase locally
- **Alcohol:** Permitted at sites
- **Firearms/Weapons:** Strictly prohibited on federal property
- **Fireworks:** Prohibited
- **Swimming:** Swim at your own risk; no lifeguards provided; pets and alcohol prohibited at beach
- **Cliff Diving:** Prohibited
- **Garbage:** Must be completely removed upon departure
- **Federal Passes:** America the Beautiful Pass, Golden Age/Golden Access Passports accepted for discounts

## 2. SALMON RUN RECREATION AREA

### Location & Contact Information
- **Location:** Off Airport Road, adjacent to U.S. Route 19 (closer to Summersville town)
- **Coordinates:** Approximately 38.20-38.21°N, 80.90°W
- **Distance from Battle Run:** Approximately 6-7 miles
- **Phone:** 304-872-3412 (Lake Office)
- **Managed by:** U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Huntington District

### Recreation Facilities
- **Camping:** DAY-USE ONLY - No overnight camping available
- **Parking:** Approximately 39 car spaces and 20 truck/trailer spaces
- **Boat Launch:** 2-lane public boat launching ramp
- **Kayak Launch:** Available for kayaks and paddle boards
- **Year-Round Access:** Only boat launch area at Summersville Lake open year-round

### Day-Use Amenities
- **Restrooms:** Vault toilet restroom facilities
- **Parking Lot:** Paved lot with vehicle/trailer spaces
- **Fishing Pier:** Available
- **Trail Access:** Multiple trailheads for hiking
- **Swimming Area:** Designated swimming area available
- **Day Use Fee:** $5.00 per vehicle

### Facilities & Access
- Kayak rental services available nearby
- Easily accessible location (off-road access)
- Popular with day-use recreators: boaters, kayakers, paddle boarders, walkers, hikers, swimmers, shoreline anglers, rock climbers
- Connects to Pirates Cove area (accessible by water or foot trail)
- Gateway to Gauley River Gorge

### Operational Notes
- Operational throughout the year (winter access available)
- Lowest water impact during seasonal drawdowns (remains accessible when lake is 3 feet below summer pool)
- Busiest season: May-October

## 3. OTHER SUMMERSVILLE LAKE USACE RECREATION AREAS

### Nine Total USACE Recreation Areas (Named)
1. **Battle Run** - Full campground (117 sites)
2. **Salmon Run** - Day-use/boat launch
3. **Long Point** - Recreation area with primitive camping (8 sites), boat ramp, overlook
4. **Damsite Picnic Area** - Day-use, picnic shelters, overlook
5-9. Six additional day-use areas (specific names vary; include wildlife management areas and access points)

### Long Point Recreation Area
- **Campsites:** 8 primitive campsites (year-round)
- **Amenities:** Boat ramp, hiking trails (Long Point Trail - 4 mile loop)
- **Features:** Scenic overlook with lake views
- **Access:** Parking available
- **Management:** May be operated by USACE or in partnership with other entities

### Damsite Picnic Area
- **Use:** Day-use only
- **Amenities:** Picnic shelters with reservation option
- **Shelter Fees:** $70.00 per shelter
- **Boat Launch:** Winter boat launch access available
- **Features:** Dam overlook viewing area

### Primitive/Dispersed Camping
- **Policy:** Shoreline and random camping strictly prohibited
- **Note:** Nearest dispersed camping option is Army Camp in New River Gorge National Park (Prince, WV), approximately 20+ miles away
- **USACE Lands:** All camping at Summersville Lake restricted to designated campgrounds only

## 7. IMPORTANT POLICIES & REGULATIONS

### Seasonal Operations
- Battle Run: Open May 1 - Columbus Day
- Water Elevation Management: Summer pool (1,652 ft) May-September; winter drawdown (1,575 ft) late fall-winter
- Peak visitation: May-September

### Federal Regulations
- All facilities governed by Title 36, United States Code (federal camping regulations)
- Enforcement of noise ordinances per local Summersville city codes (11 PM - 7 AM noise restrictions)

### Environmental
- Emerald Ash Borer Prevention: Do not bring firewood from home; purchase locally or on-site
- Wildlife Management: 6,000+ acres leased to West Virginia Division of Natural Resources

### Safety
- Swimming at own risk; no lifeguards provided
- Cliff diving banned since 2007
- All firearms/weapons strictly prohibited on federal property
- Life jackets recommended for water activities
