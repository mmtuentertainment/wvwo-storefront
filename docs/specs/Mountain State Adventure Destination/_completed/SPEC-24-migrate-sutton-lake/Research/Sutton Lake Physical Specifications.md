## Sutton Lake Physical Specifications

### Dam Operator

U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE)[^1]

### Year Completed

**1961**[^1]

### Lake Acreage

**Summer Pool:** 1,440 acres[^2][^1]

**Winter Pool:** Not found

### Shoreline Miles

**40 miles**[^1]

### Maximum Depth

**125 feet** (at the dam)[^1]

### Water Source River

**Elk River** (with contributions from the Holly River)[^1]

### Elevation

**Summer Pool:** 922 feet MSL[^3]

**Winter Pool:** 895 feet MSL[^4][^3]

### Geographic Coordinates

**38°39'43"N, 80°41'35"W**[^5]

(Decimal: 38.66204°N, 80.69315°W)[^5]

***

**Note on Construction Timeline:** While authorized by Congress in the Flood Control Act of 1938, construction began in October 1949, was interrupted by the Korean War, restarted in 1956, and was completed in 1961.[^6][^1]

**Note on Acreage Variability:** The official USACE source lists the lake at 1,440 acres, though some secondary sources cite 1,520 acres. The WVDNR officially recognizes 1,440 acres.[^2][^5][^1]
