## Sutton Lake Boat Ramps and Marinas

### USACE-Operated Boat Ramps

Sutton Lake has **four public boat launch ramps** maintained by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers:[^1]

#### 1. **Bee Run Day Use Area Boat Ramp**

- **Location:** Bee Run Recreation Area, 1734 Bee Run Rd, Sutton, WV 26601[^2]
- **Operator:** USACE
- **Ramp Conditions:** Wide concrete launch ramp[^3][^4]
- **Parking Capacity:** Large parking spaces designed for truck/trailer combinations[^5]
- **Fees:** \$3.00 day use fee per launch (credit/debit card only); Annual day use pass available for \$40[^6][^1]
- **Hours:** Not found
- **Services:** Adjacent to Sutton Lake Marina (private); swimming beach, showers, bathrooms, picnic areas and pavilions, playground[^7]
- **Restrictions:** Ramp closes when lake level drops below 917 feet[^8]


#### 2. **South Abutment Day Use Area Boat Ramp** (Behind the Dam)

- **Location:** Behind Sutton Dam[^1]
- **Operator:** USACE
- **Ramp Conditions:** Not found
- **Parking Capacity:** Not found
- **Fees:** \$3.00 day use fee per launch[^1]
- **Hours:** Not found
- **Services:** Swimming beach, picnic area with one shelter[^1]
- **Restrictions:** Not found


#### 3. **Bakers Run Campground Boat Ramp**

- **Location:** 441 Bakers Run Road, Sutton, WV 26601[^9]
- **Operator:** USACE
- **Ramp Conditions:** Boat ramp with small dock to assist with launching[^10]
- **Parking Capacity:** Accessible parking for boat trailers[^9]
- **Fees:** \$3.00 day use fee per launch; Included with campground stay[^1]
- **Hours:** Not found; Ramp open when campground is open (May 1 - September 30)[^9]
- **Services:** No permanent dock for boat storage; campground facilities include heated shower houses, dump stations, water fountains, playground[^11][^10][^1]
- **Restrictions:** Ramp closes when lake level drops below 910 feet (ramp toe exposed)[^8]


#### 4. **Gerald R. Freeman Campground Boat Ramp**

- **Location:** Gerald R. Freeman Campground, Sutton, WV 26601[^12]
- **Operator:** USACE
- **Ramp Conditions:** Accessible boat ramps[^13]
- **Parking Capacity:** Not found
- **Fees:** \$3.00 day use fee per launch; Included with campground stay[^1]
- **Hours:** Not found; Ramp open when campground is open (April 18 - November 29)[^13]
- **Services:** Small marina with snack bar on-site[^14][^15][^16][^1]
- **Restrictions:** Ramp closes when lake level drops below 912 feet (ramp toe exposed)[^8]

### Private Marina

#### **Sutton Lake Marina** (Bee Run Marina)

- **Location:** 1734 Bee Run Rd, Bee Run Recreation Area, Sutton, WV 26601[^17]
- **Operator:** Privately operated under lease from USACE[^18][^1]
- **Hours:** Open mid-April to mid-October each year (subject to water levels); 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM Monday-Saturday; Closed Sunday[^19][^17]
- **Parking Capacity:** Not found
- **Services Offered:**
    - **Fuel:** Four grades of ethanol-free marine gas[^18][^3][^19]
    - **Pump-Out:** Pump-out station (no charge for slip holders)[^4][^3]
    - **Slip Rentals:** 230 boat slips total; 100 slips with water and electric hookups[^20][^3][^18]
    - **Slip Rental Rates (2023 rates):**[^3]
        - Daily: \$25-\$40 (based on boat length up to 20', 25', 30', 35')
        - Weekly: \$95-\$150
        - Monthly: \$295-\$490
        - Seasonal: Available
        - Shore power electric and water: \$385/season (includes up to 600 kW); Daily \$10, Weekly \$45, Monthly \$125
    - **Boat Rentals:** Houseboats (3, 4, or 7 days), pontoon boats (full or half day), jet skis, kayaks (solo/tandem), stand-up paddleboards, Craig Cats, pedal boats, Paradise Pads[^19]
    - **Boat Storage:** Winter and summer boat/trailer storage, dry storage available[^21][^3][^19]
    - **Marine Services:** Marine mechanic available for repairs; winterization and shrink wrapping services[^3][^19]
    - **Ship's Store:** Pizza, hot dogs, hamburgers, hand-dipped ice cream, picnic supplies, live bait, ice, marine accessories, lake wear, souvenirs[^18][^19][^3]
    - **Other Amenities:** Clean restroom and shower facilities, laundry facility, covered picnic area, fish feeding area, wide floating docks[^19][^18]
- **Contact:** (304) 765-2120[^22][^17][^1]

**Note:** The small marina at Gerald R. Freeman Campground has a snack bar, but detailed information about fuel, pump-out, slip rentals, or operating hours at this facility was not found in official sources.[^15][^16][^14][^1]

### General USACE Information

- **Day Use Fee:** \$3.00 per vehicle for boat launching or beach access at USACE-operated areas[^1]
- **Annual Pass:** \$40 for USACE Annual Day Use Pass (valid for one calendar year)[^6][^1]
- **Discount Passes:** America the Beautiful Passes, Golden Age Passports, and Golden Access Passports receive 50% discount at USACE-operated areas[^1]
