## Sutton Lake Water Recreation Activities

### Swimming Areas

**Designated Beaches:** Swimming beaches are located at two day-use areas:[^1]

- **Bee Run Day Use Area**
- **South Abutment Day Use Area** (behind the dam)

**Restrictions and Safety:**

- No lifeguards are provided at either beach[^1]
- Swim at your own risk[^1]
- Day use fee of \$3.00 applies for beach access[^1]
- An annual day use pass (\$40) is available for frequent visitors[^1]


### Kayaking and Canoeing

**Launch Points:** Four boat launch ramps are available at:[^1]

- Bee Run Day Use Area
- Bakers Run Campground
- Gerald R. Freeman Campground
- South Abutment Day Use Area (behind the dam)

**Launch Fee:** \$3.00 day use fee per launch[^1]

**Rentals Nearby:** Kayak rentals are available at Sutton Lake Marina (privately operated under Corps lease). The marina offers 2-hour, 4-hour, and 8-hour rental periods, seven days per week, 9:00 AM–7:00 PM. Rentals include kayak, paddle, life jacket, and safety whistle.[^2][^3][^4]

### Paddleboarding

**Availability:** Stand-up paddleboard (SUP) rentals are available at Sutton Lake Marina. The marina features YOLO Hammerhead Paddle Boards (11' long, 32" wide, 260 lb. maximum weight).[^5][^3][^2]

**Rental Details:** Available seven days per week for 2, 4, or 8 hours between 9:00 AM–7:00 PM. Rentals include board, paddle, safety tether, life jacket, and safety whistle.[^5]

### Tubing

**Regulations:** Tubing is classified as "other towable or similar device" under West Virginia water skiing regulations.[^6]

**Requirements:**

- Observer at least 12 years old must be on board in a position to observe the person being towed, **OR** the vessel must be equipped with a wide-angle rear-view mirror[^6]
- Person being towed must wear a U.S. Coast Guard-approved wearable life jacket (Type I, II, or III)[^6]
- Prohibited between sunset and sunrise[^6]
- Only permitted in unlimited speed zones on government-owned reservoirs (not in no-wake zones)[^7][^6]
- Reckless or negligent operation that endangers life, limb, or property is prohibited[^6]

**Rental Restrictions:** Sutton Lake Marina explicitly states that "towing of tubes or skiers is not permitted behind any of our rental boats or jet skis".[^8]

### Water Skiing

**Permitted Areas:** Water skiing is only permitted in unlimited speed zones on Sutton Lake (a government-owned reservoir). Specific ski zone designations are not found in official sources, but general speed restrictions apply.[^7][^6]

**Requirements:**

- Skier must wear a U.S. Coast Guard-approved wearable life jacket[^9][^6]
- Observer at least 12 years old must be on board watching the skier, **OR** vessel equipped with wide-angle rear-view mirror[^10][^6]
- Prohibited between sunset and sunrise[^6]
- Self-propelled skis are not allowed in West Virginia[^9][^10]
- Skier down flag is not required in West Virginia[^10][^9]


### Jet Ski (Personal Watercraft) Rules

**Rentals Available:** Sutton Lake Marina offers jet ski (Yamaha VX Waverunner) rentals for 2, 4, or 8 hours.[^11][^2]

**Rental Requirements:**

- Renter and driver must be age 21 or older[^11]
- Maximum of 2 drivers per jet ski[^11]
- Passengers must be at least 44" tall[^11]
- Passengers under 18 must have parent/guardian present[^11]
- Valid driver's license required[^11]
- \$300 refundable damage deposit required[^11]
- Navigation limited to all of Sutton Lake up to the second railroad trestle[^11]
- No towing of tubes or people allowed behind rental jet skis[^11]

**West Virginia PWC Regulations:**

- All persons on board or being towed must wear a U.S. Coast Guard-approved wearable life jacket (Type I, II, III, or V)[^12][^6]
- Engine cutoff lanyard must be attached to operator's person, clothing, or life jacket[^6]
- Cannot operate between sunset and sunrise[^6]
- Minimum age 15 (ages 12-15 may operate with adult 18+ aboard)[^6]
- Boater education certificate required for anyone born after December 31, 1986[^6]
- Cannot operate within 100 feet of another vessel's wake at excessive speed, or within 20 feet of swimming areas, docks, or anglers[^12][^6]
- When towing water skiers/towables: must have rear-facing observer (at least 12 years old) in addition to operator; total persons cannot exceed manufacturer's maximum safe load[^13][^14]


### USACE Restrictions and Permits

**General Restrictions:**

- All water recreation activities are governed by Title 36 CFR Part 327 (Rules and Regulations Governing Public Use of Water Resource Development Projects)[^15][^1]
- Specific boating and water activity rules are available at the marina or Corps office at the dam[^1]
- No drones allowed on Corps property[^16]
- Firearms prohibited on government property[^1]
- Fireworks prohibited[^1]

**Permits Required:** No special permits are required for recreational water skiing, tubing, kayaking, paddleboarding, or jet skiing at Sutton Lake based on official sources. Standard day use fees apply (\$3.00 for boat launching and beach access).[^1]
