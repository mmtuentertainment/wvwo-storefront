## Sutton Lake Camping

### USACE-Operated Campgrounds

#### 1. **Gerald R. Freeman Campground**

- **Operator:** U.S. Army Corps of Engineers[^1][^2][^3]
- **Number of Sites:** 156 campsites[^2][^1]
    - 113 sites with electric hookups[^1]
    - 37 sites with full hookups (water, electric, sewer)[^1]
    - 6 sites non-electric[^1]
- **Amenities:**[^3][^2][^1]
    - Heated shower houses
    - Flush toilets
    - Water fountains
    - Two dump stations
    - Playgrounds
    - Basketball courts
    - Boat ramps
    - Small marina with snack bar[^4][^5][^3]
    - Accessible facilities (boat ramps, parking, picnic shelters, playgrounds)[^2]
    - Paved, level sites[^2]
- **Fees (Peak Season):**[^4]
    - Standard Electric: \$28-\$30 per night
    - Full Hookup: \$40-\$46 per night
- **Reservation System:** Reservations accepted via Recreation.gov (1-877-444-6777 or online)[^3][^2][^1]
- **Hours/Season:** Open April 18 - November 29[^2][^1]
- **Contact:** (304) 765-7756 or (304) 765-2816[^1]
- **Notes:** 14-day camping limit per 30-day period; limited cell service[^2][^1]


#### 2. **Bakers Run Campground**

- **Operator:** U.S. Army Corps of Engineers[^6][^7][^3]
- **Number of Sites:** 77 campsites (79 per some sources)[^8][^3]
    - 8 sites with full hookups (water, electric, sewer)[^8]
    - 26 sites with electric hookups[^8]
    - 43 sites non-electric[^6]
    - Approximately 20 waterfront sites[^8]
- **Amenities:**[^6][^3][^8]
    - Heated shower houses
    - Flush toilets
    - Dump stations
    - Water fountains
    - Playgrounds
    - Boat launch ramp
    - Accessible facilities (boat ramps, parking, drinking water, flush toilets, grills, playgrounds, showers)[^6]
    - Fire rings and picnic tables at each site[^8]
    - Blacktop pads[^8]
    - Extended no-wake zone[^8]
- **Fees (2022 rates):**[^9]
    - Full Hookup: \$40.00 per night
    - Standard Electric: \$30.00 per night
    - Non-Electric: \$24.00 per night
- **Peak Season Fees (2025):** \$28-\$46 per night[^6]
- **Reservation System:** Reservations accepted via Recreation.gov[^7][^6]
- **Hours/Season:** Open May 1 - September 30[^9][^3][^6]
- **Contact:** (304) 765-5631[^3]
- **Notes:** 14-day camping limit per 30-day period; pets allowed on leash[^7][^8]


#### 3. **Bee Run Campground** (Primitive)

- **Operator:** U.S. Army Corps of Engineers[^10][^11][^3]
- **Number of Sites:** 12 pull-through sites[^11][^10]
- **Amenities:**[^12][^10][^3]
    - Vault toilets (no flush toilets)
    - Fire rings
    - Lantern posts
    - Picnic tables
    - Security lighting
    - Camp host
    - **No electric hookups**
    - **No potable water**
    - **No showers**
    - **No dump station**
- **Fees (2022 rate):** \$16.00-\$18.00 per night[^13][^10]
- **Reservation System:** First-come, first-served (no reservations)[^10][^11][^3]
- **Hours/Season:** Open late May - early December (after deer season)[^10][^3]
- **Maximum RV Length:** 35 feet[^10]
- **Contact:** (304) 765-2816[^3][^10]
- **Location:** 1734 Bee Run Road, Sutton, WV 26601; hilltop location with views; lake within hiking distance or short drive to adjacent Bee Run Day Use Area[^10]
- **Notes:** 14-day camping limit per 30-day period[^10]

***

### Primitive/Dispersed Camping

**Shoreline camping is NOT permitted** at Sutton Lake.[^3]

USACE regulations specify that camping is permitted only at sites and/or areas designated by the District Commander. No dispersed or primitive camping areas beyond the three designated campgrounds above are documented in official sources.[^14][^15][^16][^17]

***

### Nearby Private Campgrounds (Within 10 Miles)

#### 1. **Flatwoods KOA Journey**

- **Location:** 350 Days Drive, Sutton, WV 26601[^18][^19][^20]
- **Distance from Sutton Lake:** Approximately 3-4 miles from Bee Run (I-79 Exit 67)[^21][^22]
- **Number of Sites:** Not specified; all sites are full hookup with 50/30 amp service[^23][^24][^21]
- **Site Types:** Long gravel pull-through sites (max 85 feet); RV sites, tent sites, and deluxe 2-bedroom/1-bathroom cabin[^21]
- **Amenities:**[^25][^23][^21]
    - Paved roads with gravel pull-throughs
    - Full hookups (water, electric, sewer) at all sites
    - Super-clean bathhouse
    - Hot showers
    - Laundry facility
    - Flush toilets
    - Free Wi-Fi
    - Playground
    - Game room
    - Hiking trails
    - Fire pits and grills
    - Indoor pool (inside Days Inn check-in office)
    - Outdoor pool (seasonal: Memorial Day - Labor Day)
    - Fitness center (at adjacent Days Inn)
    - Restaurant and tavern (at adjacent Days Inn)
    - Gift shop and business center
- **Fees:** Not found in official sources
- **Reservation/Check-in:** Check-in at adjacent Days Inn \& Suites (200-room hotel)[^23][^21]
- **Hours/Season:** Open year-round[^26][^25]
- **Contact:** (304) 765-5055 or (866) 700-7284[^19][^22][^26]
- **Website:** flatwoods.com / koa@flatwoods.com[^22]
- **Notes:** Pet-friendly (call for restrictions/fees); close to Flatwoods Outlet Mall[^25][^21]


#### 2. **Elk River Camp and RV Park**

- **Location:** 360 Little Buffalo Road (M63Q+V6), Gassaway, WV 26624[^27][^28][^29]
- **Distance from Sutton Lake:** Approximately 8-10 miles from Sutton[^27][^18]
- **Number of Sites:** Two parks: Little Buffalo and Bear Run[^29][^27]
- **Site Types:** Mix of full hookup and partial hookup sites[^30][^31][^29]
    - Full hookups: Power, water, sewer (\$40/night)[^31][^30][^29]
    - Partial hookups: Power and water (\$30/night)[^32][^29]
- **Amenities:**[^30][^29][^31]
    - Gravel pads
    - Water and electric hookups
    - **No shower house**[^32][^31]
    - **No laundry**[^31]
    - **No Wi-Fi**[^31]
    - Porta-potty available[^32]
    - **No RV dump station**[^32]
    - Located along banks of Elk River[^29][^27]
- **Fees:** \$30-\$40 per night[^30][^31][^32]
- **Hours/Season:** Not found
- **Contact:** (303) 807-5209[^28][^18]
- **Website:** elkrivercampandrv.com[^28]
- **Notes:** Reviews mention beautiful river spots but limited facilities[^31]

### General USACE Camping Policies

- **14-Day Limit:** Camping at one or more campsites for more than 14 days during any 30-consecutive-day period is prohibited without written permission from the District Commander[^16][^14][^7][^1]
- **Reservation Policies:** Campgrounds offering reservations use Recreation.gov; cancellations made at least 1 day before check-in incur a \$10 fee[^1][^6]
- **Discount Passes:** America the Beautiful Passes, Golden Age Passports, and Golden Access Passports receive 50% discount on camping fees[^3]
- **Pet Policy:** Pets allowed but must be leashed[^8]
- **Quiet Hours:** 10:00 PM - 6:00 AM[^2]
- **Firewood:** "Don't Move Firewood" policy in effect to prevent pest infestations[^1][^2]

**Contact:** Sutton Lake Project Office: (304) 765-2267 or suttonlake@usace.army.mil[^33][^3]
